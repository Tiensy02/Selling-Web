Popular dishes include Pho noodle soup, Banh Mi sandwiches, Bun Cha grilled pork with noodles, Goi Cuon spring rolls, and Com Tam broken rice dishes. Vietnamese cuisine also incorporates a variety of herbs and vegetables, including mint, cilantro, basil, and lettuce, to add depth and complexity to dishes. In addition to traditional dishes, Vietnamese cuisine has been influenced by its neighboring countries and has adopted elements of Chinese, French, and Thai cuisine. Overall, Vietnamese food offers a healthy and delicious culinary experience for food lovers around the world.
